# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Participants should:

- be respectful and constructive
- welcome different levels of experience
- focus criticism on technical work rather than people
- communicate clearly
- respect accessibility needs
- avoid harassment, discrimination and personal attacks

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment or intimidation
- discriminatory language
- publishing private information
- deliberate disruption
- abusive or insulting communication

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove or reject contributions and communications that violate these standards.

Serious concerns should be reported privately to the repository owner.
